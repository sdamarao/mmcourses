Study Question

Select the best answer.

Methods discussed in Revenue Ruling 59-60 can be used to determine which of the following?
 A. The appropriate division of an estate in the event of disputes among potential heirs.
 B. The majority stockholder in a closely held business in the event of shareholder disputes.
 C. The value stock where market quotations are readily available due to frequent trading of the stock in a public market.
 D. The value stock where quotations are available only infrequently.

You must select an answer.
There is more than one correct answer.

The division of an estate that is disputed generally is determined in court proceedings and such a decision is not necessarily guided by Revenue Ruling 59-60.

The majority stockholder is determined by the percentage of shares owned by each individual. Revenue Ruling 59-60 does not provide guidance for this determination.

The methods discussed in Revenue Ruling 59-60 can be used when valuing stock where market quotations are unavailable. When stock is traded frequently on the public market, the guidelines discussed in Revenue Ruling 59-60 are not necessary.

The correct answer is D.

The methods discussed in Revenue Ruling 59-60 can be used when valuing stock where market quotations occur so infrequently that they do not reflect fair market value.
