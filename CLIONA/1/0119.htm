Study Question

Select the best answer.

Which of the following statements is false?
 A. A firm that serves a regional market is more likely to be comparable to a typical closely held firm.
 B. Firms that produce a single product tend to be more comparable to the typical closely held firm.
 C. Firms that use the issuance of bonds as a financing source tend to be more comparable to the typical closely held firm.
 D. Smaller firms tend to be more comparable to the typical closely held firm.

You must select an answer.
There is more than one correct answer.

As a general rule, a firm that serves a regional market rather than a national or an international customer group is more likely to be comparable to a typical closely held firm.

As a general rule, firms that produce a full range of products rather than a single product do not tend to be more comparable to the typical closely held firm since small firms typically produce a single or limited number of products.

As a general rule, smaller firms, rather than larger firms, tend to be comparable to the typical closely held company.

The correct answer is C.

Most small, closely held firms do not have access to the bond market. Therefore, firms that use bond financing tend not to be good comparables for closely held firms.
