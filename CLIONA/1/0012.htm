As with any type of consulting engagement, a CPA accepting a valuation engagement needs to have the following:

They must have Professional Competence and accept only engagements that the individual (or firm) can expect to complete with the required specialized proficiency. A business valuation certification as well as the required CPE necessary to retain those certifications provides a CPA with the basis for the necessary competency.

They must perform all business valuations with Due Professional Care. SSVS No. 1 provides an outline of the steps necessary when executing a business valuation.

They must Plan and Supervise the business valuation in an adequate fashion. Again, SSVS No. 1 provides a guideline to help accomplish these goals.

They must obtain Sufficient Relevant Data to support the determination of value. SSVS No. 1 clearly spells out the type of information that is necessary to support a calculated or conclusion of value.
