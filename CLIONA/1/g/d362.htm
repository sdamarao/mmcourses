Property

In the strict legal sense, property is all rights relating to ownership and the resultant control of things tangible and intangible, guaranteed and protected by law, which may include the following:

  • Realty (real property)—land and anything erected on, growing on, or permanently attached to the land (immovables)

  • Personalty (personal property)—everything that is the subject of ownership that is not real property (movables)
