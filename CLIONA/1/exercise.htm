Practice Exercises

Some courses will contain additional learning exercises to help you practice certain skills. In these Practice Exercises you may be asked to "drag and drop" words from one area of the screen to another, match lists of terms to their definitions, or put events or actions in the appropriate sequence. You will always be provided with the correct responses after you have completed these ungraded exercises.

Study Questions

The study questions in each chapter will help you review the material and will prepare you for the exam. Read the study question, select or enter your answer choice(s), then select the Submit Answer button to receive feedback. If your answer was incorrect, you may try again or move onto the next study question. When you are ready to move on, select the Next button.

There are several types of study questions.

  • Multiple Choice

    • Select one answer from a set of answer choices, usually labeled A, B, C, and D, by selecting on the circle next to the letter of your choice. (Circles indicate there is only one correct answer choice.)

  • Pick-All-That-Are-True

    • This type of question offers a set of answer choices of which at least two are correct. Select all the choices you know to be correct by selecting on their boxes, and checkmarks will appear in the boxes. (Boxes indicate there is more than one correct answer choice.) Select again on a box if you wish to uncheck and withdraw that answer choice.
