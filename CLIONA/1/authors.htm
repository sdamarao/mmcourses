Jill Hazelbauer-Von der Ohe, Retired , CPA, MBA, CVA, CFM, has her MBA from Rockford College and has taught at Agnes Scott College and Rockford College as well as Keller and DeVry universities. She is a CPA and has also received certifications as a CMA and CFM, having received the Gold Award for the CFM exam during the 97-98 winter exam cycle. In addition, she holds a CVA (Certified Valuation Analyst), a professional group that focuses on the valuation of closely held businesses for tax, estate, and other purposes. She has worked on accounting projects in Uganda and Poland.


Tara Lamphier, CPA is an A&A manager at a local CPA firm. She is a member of the AICPA and the Rhode Island Society of CPAs. She is also an author and technical editor of CPE courses, as well as a writer of review and exam questions for self-study courses. Tara has served on committees for the AICPA and Massachusetts Society of CPAs, including a committee to develop the grading system for the Auditing and Attestation portion of the computerized uniform CPA examination.
