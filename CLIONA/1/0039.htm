Peter, CPA is trying to determine whether he has the professional competence to perform a particular business valuation. He would need to consider which of the following items when making that determination?
 A. If limiting conditions were imposed by the client
 B. If Peter has performed attest services for the client in the past
 C. Peter's understanding of governmental regulations that apply to the engagement
 D. The nature, purpose, and object of the valuation engagement

You must select an answer.
There is more than one correct answer.

This is important to determining if the valuator will accept the engagement, not if the valuator has the professional competence to perform the valuation.

This is a key consideration when determining if a conflict of interest exists, but not if the valuator is competent in performing the valuation.

This is one of the items that should be part of a written understanding between the CPA and the client, not a determination of professional competency.

The correct answer is C.

Governmental regulations can have a significant impact on any business valuation. An understanding of how those regulations will apply to the engagement is essential to a valuation analyst.
