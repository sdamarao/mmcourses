Online Searches

A leading set of sources for finding potential guidelines would include online databases and searches of the industry. Websites and industry association publications provide a wealth of information that can be useful when conducting a search for such firms. Although some sources will supply little more than company names, it is possible—through additional searching or through the use of more sophisticated databases—to find information about potential guidelines, such as location, sales, solvency, and net income. It is often desirable to visit the website of a potential guideline organization in order to obtain more detailed information.

Using the information from these sources, it is then possible to develop a list of publicly traded companies that are in a similar business. It is from this list that an attempt to find guideline public firms needs to be made.
