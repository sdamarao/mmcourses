Study Question

Select the best answer.

Courts routinely require the use of which of the following?
 A. Fewer guideline companies if comparability is found to be extremely similar to the subject company
 B. One very close guideline company when using guidelines to value a business
 C. Seven to ten guideline firms before a method of business valuation based on the use of comparables can be employed
 D. Valuation methods that do not use guideline companies

You must select an answer.
There is more than one correct answer.

In the case Estate of Joyce C. Hall, the courts specifically stated that only one guideline organization is not acceptable for a valuation.

The courts do not require the use of a specific number of comparables. The quality of the comparables is considered more than the quantity.

Although courts may require a particular valuation method to be used, they do not routinely disallow the use of market methods of valuation.

The correct answer is A.

The more similar the guideline companies are to the subject organization being valued, the fewer the number of guideline organizations needed.
