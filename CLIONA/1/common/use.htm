How to use this course


Here are a few quick tips to get you started.

For simple navigation through the course, use the Next (>) and Previous (<) buttons in the bottom right corner of the screen. They will take you to the next and the previous screen.

When you are ready to quit, use the Quit button. The course will remember where you left off. When you resume studying, the course will open to the last screen you saw.

Your progress through each lesson is displayed in the bubble in the upper right corner of the screen. To navigate to any lesson or section, use the Table of Contents on the right side of the screen.

For more complete information, consult Help in the menu bar at the top of the screen
