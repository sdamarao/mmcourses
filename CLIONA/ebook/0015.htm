The Statement on Standards of Business Valuation No. 1 (SSVS No. 1) was developed to provide all except which of the following?
 
correct

correct

correct

C. Guidelines to use in the preparation of a business valuation by CPAs.
 
correct

D. The promotion of transparency in business valuation engagements prepared by CPAs.

You must select an answer.
There is more than one correct answer.

Unlike the two CPAs involved in the Berg case who did not have formal education in the area of business valuation, CPAs who can provide proof of competency as required by SSVS No. 1 do gain credibility in both the legal setting and the business community when performing a business valuation as the result of a higher quality and improved consistency in the business valuations prepared.

In a news release when SSVS No. 1 was issued in 2007, it was stated that the standards “provides our members with a set of guidelines in the unique context of a CPA practice”.

In a news release when SSVS No. 1 was issued in 2007, it was stated that: “the standard promotes greater transparency” in business valuations.

The correct answer is A.

Revenue Ruling 59-60 and not SSVS No. 1 presented a general approach for business valuation including the presentation of a general method to use when valuing the shares of stock of a closely held corporation for estate and gift tax purposes.
