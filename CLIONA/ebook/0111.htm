Issues

If this were all there was to finding guideline organizations, the process would be time-consuming but manageable.

Issues arise when qualitative factors are examined. Some of these factors involve a more in-depth examination of the financial information, and others require subjective judgment. Many of the issues described in the remainder of this chapter might force the business valuator to reject one or more of possible firms deemed to be in a similar line of business as the subject company as a guideline organization.
