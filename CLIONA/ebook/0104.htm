  • Quick ratio = 2.4

  • Times interest earned = 9.8

  • Price/earnings = 13

  • Debt/equity = 2.6

There is more than one correct answer.

The cash and the P/E ratio would need to be related in order to calculate a reasonable value.

Interest is not subtracted from after tax earnings to calculate a reasonable value.

The cost basis of the debt value is not multiplied by the P/E ratio in order to determine value. They are not related.

The correct answer is C.

Earnings times P/E ratio equals value: $325,000 × 13 = $4,225,000.
