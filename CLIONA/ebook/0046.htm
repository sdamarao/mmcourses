Market approach

A market approach seeks market comparisons in order to determine the value of a closely held business. This valuation method is preferred by the IRS and courts, so a search for comparables should be performed. However, it can be difficult—and often impossible—to find useful guideline companies or comparables.

The use of guideline companies when valuing a closely held business will be discussed in more detail later in the course. Please note that “comparable” and “guideline” are considered to be similar terms; however, the term “guideline” has become the preferred name in recent years.
