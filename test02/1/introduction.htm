Introduction

We designed this Checkpoint Learning CPE course to help you master the subject matter in an easy, efficient manner.


As you work your way through each chapter, you will encounter study questions that check your comprehension of the preceding material. Some courses will also contain practice exercises to help you apply certain concepts. At the end of the course you will be given an exam. You must pass this exam in order to pass the course and obtain your CPE certificate. (You will not be graded on study questions or practice exercises.)


While we recommend that you work your way through the chapters in the order they appear, you may choose to go to any chapter at any time by opening the Course Outline box and selecting the desired chapter.


Note: Since new accounting and auditing standards, tax laws, and regulations may have a substantial impact upon the content of this course, you are allowed one year from the date of purchase to complete the course and all test questions.
