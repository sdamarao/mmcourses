Exam

An exam will be presented at the end of the course. The exam questions will be multiple choice.



You may move among the questions by selecting the Next or Back buttons or by selecting a particular question from the drop-down box provided. You have the option of returning to any question to change your answer before selecting the Grade My Answers button at the end of the course. Note that you may move back and forth among the test questions and change your answers up until the time you select Grade My Answers. At this time, no more changes are allowed and your answers will be judged.



Once you have answered the exam questions, the Submit Answers button will become active. This button is located in the lower right corner of the page, above the Next and Back buttons.



In order to receive CPE credit, you must pass the exam with a cumulative score of 70% or higher. Unlike study questions, due to NASBA policies, you will not receive any remedial or reinforcement feedback for test questions.



Once you pass the course, you will be shown all test questions along with your answers and, where applicable, the correct answer.



Additionally, once you pass the course, you cannot retake the exam to improve your score.



You may take exam at any time by selecting it in the Course Outline. For more information please contact Checkpoint Learning support.
(http://support.rg.thomsonreuters.com/800support.asp)



You have one year from the date you purchase the course to pass the course for CPE credit.
