What information could be gathered during the planning phase?
 A. All federal laws and regulations
 B. Financial information
 C. Personnel evaluations
 D. Board minutes

The correct answer is B.

Financial information, both internal and external, should be readily available and gathered during the planning phase. Such financial data could include financial statements (balance sheet, income statement, and statement of cash flow) as well budget versus actual reports, sales statistics, and customer statistics and so forth. Such information can quickly inform the reviewer as to the relative size of functions within the company and indications of critical issues.
