Theresa A. Johnson, CPA is a CPE Senior Technical Editor and writer for the Thomson Reuters Business Compliance and Knowledge Solutions team. She earned her B.A. from Creighton University in Omaha, Nebraska, has continued her accounting and business education at Midwestern State University and Baylor University in Texas, and is certified in Texas. Her extensive accounting, technical, and publishing experience includes positions as comptroller for a graphics design agency, systems administrator, senior editor, and project manager for various publishing companies, and several years as a self-employed tax practitioner and computer and software consultant.
