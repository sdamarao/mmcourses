5. Operational Review Triangle

The three Es of operational review—economy, efficiency, and effectiveness—or what may be referred to as operational review triangle are graphically presented in Exhibit 1.1.

note

The operational review process typically starts by analyzing the organizations planning systems—that is, to determine the agreed upon reasons why the organization is in existence and the related goals and objectives established by management to move them effectively in those directions. The budget system is looked at as the manifestation of the planning process by allocating scarce resources to the achievement of planning systems goals and objectives—encompassing the three Es.
