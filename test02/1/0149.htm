The correct answer is A.

Financial ratios provide adequate perspective as to the results of the company's finances as well as the results of operations. They are quick and excellent tools by which to analyze the company's financial statements. For instance, the direction of inventory turnovers may provide a quick indication of whether the company is working its way out of the inventory business as desired or getting more heavily burdened with inventory.
