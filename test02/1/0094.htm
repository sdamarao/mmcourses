Chapter 2. Planning Phase

Upon completion of this chapter, the user should be able to:

  • discuss the purpose of the planning phase in an operational review,

  • list information that must be obtained during the planning phase and related sources of information,

  • explain how to use planning phase information in the identification of critical operational areas,

  • define planning and budget concepts and their expansion into operational areas and related principles of good operational controls, and

  • describe a sample operational review planning phase work program.
