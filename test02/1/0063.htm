Personnel and Staffing

  • Does an organizational chart exist? If so, provide copy.

  • Do functional job descriptions exist for each block on the organization chart? If so, provide copies.

  • Do staffing statistics by functional area exist? If so, provide copy.

  • Is there a system of employee evaluations? Describe.

  • How are employees recruited, hired, and fired?

  • How are employees oriented and trained?

  • What are promotional policies?

  • How are raises determined?

  • Is there a grievance mechanism?

  • What type of personnel records is maintained?

Management

  • Who is considered top management? Provide list of names.

  • Who is considered middle management? Provide list of names.

  • How adequate are existing reports in furnishing information for making management decisions?

  • Are there internal downward communication tools to the staff?

  • Is authority effectively delegated to management and lower levels?
